About Our School

Established in 1980, RAJKIYEKRIT +2 HIGH SCHOOL MAI is a Government Higher Secondary School managed by the Department of Education, Government of Bihar. Located in Mai, Hilsa Block, Nalanda District, Bihar, the school provides quality education from Class IX to Class XII in a safe, disciplined, and student-friendly environment. With Hindi as the medium of instruction, the school is committed to academic excellence, character building, and the overall development of students. The campus is equipped with essential facilities, including a government school building, electricity, safe drinking water, separate toilets for boys and girls, and a library containing nearly 950 books. Our mission is to nurture knowledgeable, responsible, and confident learners by providing quality education and encouraging values, discipline, and lifelong learning. We strive to prepare every student for higher education and a bright future.

Principal's Message

Education is the foundation of a successful life and the key to building a progressive society. At RAJKIYEKRIT +2 HIGH SCHOOL MAI, we are committed to providing quality education that inspires students to achieve academic excellence, develop strong moral values, and become responsible citizens of the nation. We believe that every child possesses unique talents, and our responsibility is to nurture those talents with care, dedication, and guidance.

Our school provides a safe, disciplined, and student-friendly learning environment where knowledge is combined with creativity, innovation, and character building. Along with academic excellence, we encourage students to participate in sports, cultural activities, social service, and various co-curricular programs that help develop leadership, confidence, teamwork, and communication skills.

Our dedicated teachers work tirelessly to ensure that every student receives personal attention and the best possible guidance. We believe that education is not merely about securing good marks in examinations but about developing honesty, discipline, compassion, critical thinking, and a lifelong passion for learning. Together with parents and the community, we strive to prepare our students to face future challenges with confidence and integrity.

I warmly welcome all students, parents, and visitors to the official website of RAJKIYEKRIT +2 HIGH SCHOOL MAI. Let us work together to create an inspiring educational journey that empowers every learner to achieve success, contribute positively to society, and make our nation proud.
